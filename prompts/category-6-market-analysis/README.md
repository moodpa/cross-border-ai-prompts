## Category 6: Market Analysis (5 prompts)
### Prompt 36: Store Audit
```
Act as an eCommerce consultant. Audit this store/brand: [store URL or name].
Analyze:
- Traffic estimation and primary channels
- Product mix and pricing strategy
- Brand positioning and messaging
- Customer experience (site design, checkout flow, mobile optimization)
- Social media presence and engagement
- Estimated monthly revenue range
- Top 3 growth opportunities
Identify quick wins (implement in 1 week) and strategic moves (1-3 months).
```
### Prompt 37: Market Sizing Model
```
Build a bottom-up market sizing model for [product category] in [target market (US/EU/etc.)].
Include:
- Total addressable market (TAM)
- Serviceable addressable market (SAM)
- Serviceable obtainable market (SOM)
- Key assumptions and data sources
- Growth rate projections (1, 3, 5 years)
- Seasonal fluctuations
- Regional variations within the market
Format as clear numbers with rationale for each estimate.
```
### Prompt 38: Competitor Monitor Setup
```
Create a competitor monitoring framework for tracking [competitor name/brand].
What to track weekly:
- New product launches
- Price changes
- Advertising activity (new ads, creatives)
- Social media changes (followers, engagement, content)
- Review trends (new reviews, rating changes)
- Website changes (design, copy, offers)
Set up alerts for: major price drops, stockouts, new best-sellers, policy changes.
```
### Prompt 39: Category White Space Analysis
```
Analyze the [product category] on Amazon/eBay/Etsy for white space opportunities.
Methodology:
1. List top 50 products by revenue
2. Identify common patterns (country of origin, price point, features)
3. Find customer complaints in 3-star and below reviews
4. Identify "if only it had X" patterns in reviews
5. Cross-reference with Google Trends rising queries
6. Suggest 5 product concepts that address unmet needs
Each concept: specific product idea, target price point, estimated demand level.
```
### Prompt 40: International Market Prioritization
```
I'm based in China selling [product type]. Which international markets should I prioritize?
Evaluate for each market (US, UK, EU, Japan, Southeast Asia, Australia, Middle East, Latin America):
- Market size and growth rate
- Logistical complexity (shipping, customs, duties)
- Regulatory barriers (certifications, labeling, restrictions)
- Competitive intensity
- Price sensitivity and acceptable margins
- Payment preferences
- Cultural considerations
Score each market (1-10) and recommend top 3 to enter first.
```
