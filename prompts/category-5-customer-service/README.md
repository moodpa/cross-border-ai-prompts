## Category 5: Customer Service (6 prompts)
### Prompt 30: Multi-language Support Template
```
Act as a customer service translator. Convert this English response into [language]:
[English text]
The translation must:
- Maintain the same helpful, professional tone
- Use culturally appropriate phrases for [target market]
- Avoid literal translations that sound unnatural
- Preserve all key information (order details, policy references)
- Include a disclaimer about AI translation if needed
Languages available: Spanish, German, French, Japanese, Korean (specify which).
```
### Prompt 31: Refund/Return Handling Script
```
Draft a professional, empathetic response to a customer requesting a refund for [product]. Reason given: "[customer's reason]".
The response should:
1. Acknowledge their frustration empathetically
2. Apologize sincerely (not defensively)
3. Explain the return/refund process clearly
4. Offer alternatives if applicable (replacement, store credit, discount)
5. Maintain a relationship-preserving tone
Also prepare: 3 "save the sale" alternatives to a full refund.
```
### Prompt 32: FAQ Auto-Response Builder
```
Create a comprehensive FAQ auto-response system for [product type/category].
For each of the top 15 customer questions, provide:
- The question (as customer would ask it)
- A clear, complete answer (50-100 words)
- A short version (1-2 sentences for quick replies)
- Category tags for routing
Cover: shipping, sizing/measurements, materials, care instructions, returns, tracking, stock availability.
```
### Prompt 33: Escalation Handler
```
A customer is escalating an issue: [describe escalation scenario]. Draft a response to de-escalate and resolve.
The response must:
- Validate their emotions without admitting fault prematurely
- Take ownership of the resolution process
- Be specific about next steps and timeline
- Offer a concrete goodwill gesture
- Create a record trail for further escalation if needed
Also write internal notes for what to check/verify before responding.
```
### Prompt 34: Positive Review Thank You
```
Draft a response to a 5-star review for [product].
The response:
- Thanks the customer by first name
- Mentions one specific detail from their review (shows you read it)
- Offers a related tip or suggestion
- Politely invites them to check other products
Keep under 100 words. For Amazon, keep under 500 characters per TOS.
```
### Prompt 35: Negative Review Recovery
```
Draft a response to a negative review for [product]. Issue: "[customer complaint]".
The response:
1. Apologize for their experience (specific to their issue)
2. Explain what happened (if known) without making excuses
3. Offer a specific resolution (refund, replacement, help)
4. Invite them to contact you directly for faster resolution
5. Thank them for their feedback (shows you value improvement)
Key: other potential customers will read this. Show you're responsive and fair.
```
