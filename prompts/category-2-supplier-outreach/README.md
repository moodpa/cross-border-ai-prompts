## Category 2: Supplier Outreach (6 prompts)
### Prompt 9: Cold Email to Supplier
```
Draft a professional cold email to a supplier on Alibaba/1688. I'm looking to source [product type] in [quantity] quantity.
The email should:
- Be polite but direct
- Show I've done my research (mention their specific product/specialty)
- Ask for: MOQ, unit price, sample cost, production lead time
- Include my business background briefly
- End with a clear call-to-action
Tone: professional, respectful, serious buyer. Keep under 150 words.
```
### Prompt 10: Price Negotiation Script
```
You are a master negotiator for cross-border sourcing. I need to negotiate a better price with [supplier name] for [product]. Current quote: $[X]/unit at [Y] MOQ.
Draft a negotiation reply that:
1. Thanks them for the quote (maintain relationship)
2. Mentions a competitor's comparable quote (even if slightly higher)
3. Proposes a volume commitment in exchange for better pricing
4. Asks for specific concessions (MOQ reduction, improved packaging, better payment terms)
5. Leaves room for their counter-offer
Include 3 alternative deal structures I can offer.
```
### Prompt 11: Quality Control Checklist Generator
```
Create a comprehensive QC checklist for inspecting [product type] before shipment.
Include:
- Visual inspection criteria
- Functional testing procedures
- Material quality benchmarks
- Packaging and labeling requirements
- Measurement tolerances
- Sampling methodology (AQL levels)
- Documentation requirements
Format as a printable checklist the inspection team can use.
```
### Prompt 12: Supplier Comparison Matrix
```
I'm evaluating [3+] suppliers for [product type]. Help me create a comparison framework with weighted scoring.
Scoring criteria (weight %):
- Price competitiveness (20%)
- Quality/reputation (25%)
- Communication & responsiveness (15%)
- Lead time (15%)
- Payment terms (10%)
- MOQ flexibility (10%)
- Certifications (5%)
For each supplier, list specific questions to ask and red flags to investigate.
```
### Prompt 13: RFQ (Request for Quotation) Template
```
Draft a professional RFQ for [product type]. I need to send this to multiple suppliers for comparison.
Include:
- Product specifications (dimensions, materials, colors, features)
- Packaging requirements (retail packaging, barcode, inserts)
- Quality standards and certifications needed
- Quantity ranges and pricing tiers
- Incoterms options (FOB, CIF, EXW)
- Payment terms preferences
- Timeline expectations
- Sample requirements
Leave [brackets] for me to fill in specific numbers.
```
### Prompt 14: Supplier Relationship Nurture
```
I've been working with [supplier name] for [time period]. Help me draft a message to:
1. Order reorder of [product] with potential modifications
2. Ask about new products they've developed
3. Discuss exclusive arrangements
4. Inquire about pre-production samples for new season
Goal: strengthen the relationship while getting better terms. What "value" can I offer them beyond just orders?
```
